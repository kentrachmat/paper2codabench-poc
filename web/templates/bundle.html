What are Verification Seals?

Verification seals provide cryptographic proof that:

  • Authenticity: The bundle and results are genuine
  • Integrity: Files haven't been tampered with (SHA-256 hash)
  • Timestamp: When the bundle/evaluation was created
  • Traceability: Results can be linked back to specific runs

Each seal uses RSA-2048 digital signatures - a simplified "blockchain-lite" approach for POC verification.
